Product
How does ScamCheck work? ▾
You paste any suspicious message — text, email, DM — and our AI analyzes it against 50+ known scam patterns. You get a verdict (Scam / Suspicious / Legit), a risk score, and specific red flags detected from your message.
How accurate is it? ▾
Our model correctly identifies scams with over 94% accuracy based on internal testing across 50,000+ messages. When in doubt, always verify through official channels.
Is my data private? ▾
Yes. We never store the content of messages you submit. Each check is processed in real time and discarded immediately. We never sell your data.
What types of scams does it detect? ▾
IRS impersonation, bank phishing, romance scams, job fraud, package delivery scams, tech support scams, prize/lottery fraud, AI voice clone scripts, crypto investment scams, and more.
